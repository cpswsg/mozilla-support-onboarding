About SUMO

MOzilla SUpport, SUMO for short, helps millions of users get the most out of their favourite browser. It is a community made up of enthusiastic volunteers and employees trying to support our users around the globe. As promoters of the free and open web, we empower global knowledge share, technical help and community mentorship.


Used by more than 400 million people, Mozilla support is fully powered by volunteers.
